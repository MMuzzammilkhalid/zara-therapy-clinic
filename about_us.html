About Us - Zara Therapy Clinic

Established in 2006 by Dr. Wade Brackenbury and a dedicated team of associates, Zara Therapy Clinic has location in Ho Chi Minh City with modern facilities to provide international standards of healthcare services.



Dr. Wade Brackenbury

Our mission
We provide therapy at the highest standard of quality in private room one-on-one care, with practices in a comfortable and professional environment, and ensure that our healthcare solutions meet your individual needs.
We are committed as primary healthcare professionals to help our clients resume their lifelong pursuit of health, fitness, and well-being.
Our Philosophy
Physio Physical Therapy is committed to providing high quality outpatient physical therapy services in beautiful state-of-the-art facilities.
As a healthcare leader we partner with groups and events within the communities we serve. Our focus on our physical therapists continuing education is unmatched and guarantees our physical therapists understand and put into practice cutting-edge treatments and rehabilitation techniques available for all types of injuries and conditions.
Foreign doctors team
Our doctors from the United States, Canada, France, New Zealand and South Korea are well qualified, experienced, dedicated to the health of patients. We are among one of a few high quality medical establishments in Vietnam that regularly attracts overseas patients from Asian and Western countries.
